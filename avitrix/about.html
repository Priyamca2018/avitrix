About Our Company

Mr. Bingle is experienced panel beater in Melbourne reputed for its quality workmanship. We are prestige car specialists passionate about giving a new look to the luxury cars that have met with an accident. Our technicians are expert prestige car specialist who provides excellent smash/crash repair services and insurance assistance to car owners. With dual service under a single roof, you can get back your car like the way it was before the accident with approved repair works according to the standards set by car manufacturers as well as insurance companies. We are expert in no-fault accident repairs and hassle-free claims. That’s why we are the most sought after panel beaters in Melbourne trusted by car owners as well as insurance companies.


Since each insurance company has its own guidelines for claim approval, we keep ourselves updated with those guidelines. Our expert technical team ensures that we are equipped with the latest and advanced technology & industrial tools. In addition, our workforce keeps updating their knowledge and skills related to advanced engineering systems installed in new age premium cars. Our expertise & in-depth knowledge of engineering technology used in latest prestige cars is an added advantage to car owners. We use only certified techniques, tools, and information to ensure your vehicle will be taken care of properly.


With state-of-the-art technologies and tools at our body shop, customer satisfaction is guaranteed. Mr. Bingle mechanics use high-tech skills to inspect, maintain, and repair luxury & premium cars. We are skilled and equipped to cater for all prestige car makes and models. We are experienced in restoring & repairing prestige cars of all makes and models to their pre-accident stage. Having worked with most insurance companies over the years, we are confident of our skills in meeting all the parameters for accident claims and no-fault repairs and our clients don’t face any issues with insurance claims.
